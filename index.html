About us

The purpose of CXI is to promote and Organize project skills and experience at MIET. The club aims to bring in collaboration from the students so as to build products Or provide services that solve a common problem or give shape to an innovative idea while working in teams and learning with experience.

We are CXI.The pragmatic and progressive Computer Science Club of MIET. We bring a more sophisticated approach to student centered college bodies along with transparency in governance.
